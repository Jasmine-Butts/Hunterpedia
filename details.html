Killua Zoldyck

Background

Killua Zoldyck is one of the main protagonists of Hunter x Hunter. He is a member of the Zoldyck family, a powerful and wealthy family known for their assassins. Despite his family's dark reputation, Killua is kind-hearted and has a strong sense of justice.


Abilities

Killua Zoldyck is a skilled assassin and has the ability to use his "Nen" powers. His main abilities include his signature "Nen" technique, "Zoldyck Family's Secret Technique", and his ability to manipulate his "Nen" energy to enhance his physical abilities.
